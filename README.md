# Zomato-Bangalore-Sales-Performance-Dashboard
This Power BI dashboard analyzes restaurant trends, customer preferences, and market performance across various localities in Bangalore. The report is divided into four main sections:

---

## 1. Customer Preferences

- Vegetarian Restaurants (Bar Chart): Malleshwaram, HSR, and Indiranagar have the highest number of vegetarian-only restaurants.
- Dinner Ratings (Line Chart): Pizza Hut, Chai Point, and McDonald's received the most dinner ratings.
- Takeaway Availability (Azure Map): Map visualizing the geographic distribution of restaurants with and without takeaway options.
- Average Cost by Area (Line Chart): Cumulative restaurant costs by area — useful to identify high-cost zones.
- Delivery Reviews by Area (Tree Map): HSR, BTM, and Marathahalli lead in volume of delivery reviews.

---

## 2. Restaurant Performance

- Cuisine Type (Donut Chart): South Indian and North Indian cuisines are the most common.
- Average Cost by Brand (Bar Chart): Highlights costly restaurant brands like Starbucks and Cafe Coffee Day.
- Indoor Seating Availability (Scatter Plot): Banashankari and Bellandur rank high in indoor seating options.
- Feature Totals (Gauge Chart): Combined total of indoor seating, veg-only, and home delivery features: 3950 (Range: 645–8904).
- "Known For" Tags (Bar % Chart): Equal representation across categories like Cafe, Desserts, and Street Food (100% each).

---

## 3. Market Analysis

- Cost & Review Metrics (Table): Shows total and individual values of average cost and delivery reviews.
- Deliveries per Day (KPI): Total daily deliveries across areas: 137,220.
- Natural Language Q&A Box: Enables users to ask data-related questions like "count timing" or "total is veg only".
- Restaurants by Area (Pie Chart): Electronic City, Marathahalli, and HSR have the most restaurants.
- Top Restaurants (Table): Lists popular and high-cost restaurants like The Crowne and RJ 14.

---

## 4. Comparative & Benchmarking Analysis

- Delivery vs Dinner Ratings (Scatter Plot): Analyzes correlation between dine-in experience and delivery satisfaction.
- Average Delivery Ratings by Area (Bar Chart): Windmill Craftworks and VR Bengaluru lead in customer satisfaction.
- Dinner Ratings by Cuisine (Line Chart): South Indian, Chinese, and North Indian cuisines top the list.
- Average Cost Comparison (Bar Chart): Shanti Nagar and Hilton Road are premium pricing zones.
- Restaurant Count by Cost & Area (Stacked Area Chart): Shows restaurant density spikes around mid-range pricing.
